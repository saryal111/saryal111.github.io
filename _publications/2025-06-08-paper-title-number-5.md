This study evaluates the specific energy performance of a fertilizer-based liquid desiccant concept that uses cold concentrated fertilizer solution as the drying agent, closing the water cycle through fertigation and eliminating energy-intensive desiccant regeneration for sustainable greenhouse cultivation.
